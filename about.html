Our Mission

Risk Runners is dedicated to democratizing corporate risk intelligence by providing comprehensive, accessible, and actionable risk analysis for publicly traded companies. We believe that understanding risk factors is crucial for making informed investment decisions.

Our platform transforms complex SEC filings and regulatory documents into intuitive, searchable insights that help investors, analysts, and researchers identify potential risks and opportunities across thousands of companies.

Background

Risk Runners was created to address the challenge of making sense of the vast amount of risk information contained in corporate SEC filings. Traditional financial analysis often focuses on historical performance metrics, but understanding forward-looking risk factors is equally important for investment decisions.

Our platform leverages natural language processing and data analysis techniques to extract meaningful insights from regulatory filings, particularly focusing on the risk factor sections of 10-K annual reports filed around 2019.

The project is part of a broader ecosystem of analytical tools designed to help investors and researchers make more informed decisions by providing better access to corporate risk intelligence.
